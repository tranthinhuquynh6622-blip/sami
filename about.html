About EduTransform

Revolutionizing education through the power of artificial intelligence and interactive storytelling

Our Mission

"To unlock human potential by transforming passive learning into active, engaging experiences that inspire curiosity and foster lifelong learning."

At EduTransform, we believe that education should be as captivating as it is informative. Every student deserves learning experiences that adapt to their needs, spark their imagination, and prepare them for a rapidly changing world.

Our AI-powered platform bridges the gap between traditional educational materials and the interactive, personalized learning experiences that today's digital natives expect and deserve.

Cognitive Science

Active Learning

Adaptive Systems

Our Journey

2020

Founded

EduTransform was founded by Dr. Maria Santos, a former educator and AI researcher, with a vision to make learning more engaging through technology.

2021

First Prototype

Launched our beta product with 50 pilot schools, proving that AI-powered content transformation could significantly improve student engagement.

2022

Series A Funding

Secured $15M in Series A funding to expand our AI research team and scale the platform to serve institutions nationwide.

2023

Global Expansion

Expanded to serve educators in 45 countries, reaching over 1 million students and establishing partnerships with major LMS platforms.

2024

Industry Recognition

Named "EdTech Innovation of the Year" and reached 2 million students worldwide with our most advanced AI engine yet.
