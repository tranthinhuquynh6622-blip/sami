Best Practices

Expert recommendations for maximizing educational impact

01

Start Small, Scale Gradually

Begin by transforming one lesson or module. Use the analytics to understand what works, then expand to more content. This iterative approach leads to better outcomes and less overwhelm.

  • Choose high-impact topics first
  • Gather student feedback early
  • Build on successful patterns
02

Leverage Analytics for Continuous Improvement

Use the built-in analytics to identify where students struggle. Look for patterns in quiz performance, time spent on sections, and drop-off points to optimize your content.

  • Review weekly performance reports
  • Identify knowledge gaps proactively
  • A/B test different narrative approaches
03

Balance Story with Substance

While narratives are engaging, ensure learning objectives remain clear. Use stories to contextualize and motivate, but always connect back to core concepts and skills.

  • Map narrative to learning outcomes
  • Include knowledge checkpoints
  • Provide summary references
04

Personalize the Experience

Use the adaptive features to cater to different learning styles and paces. Allow students to choose their path through content while ensuring all learning objectives are met.

  • Enable adaptive difficulty settings
  • Offer multiple content pathways
  • Provide remediation loops
